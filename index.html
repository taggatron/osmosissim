Method Notes

  • Each cylinder starts close to 5 g to mimic matched potato cores.
  • Lower sucrose concentrations usually cause a gain in mass.
  • Higher sucrose concentrations usually cause a loss in mass.
  • Formula used: ((final - initial) / initial) x 100.
  • The isotonic point is where the graph crosses 0% change in mass.
