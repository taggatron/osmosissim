What is happening?

Osmosis is the movement of water molecules through a semi-permeable membrane. Water likes to go where there is less water and more "stuff" (like sugar or salt) to try and even things out!

💡 Try this: Add lots of sugar to the RIGHT side and none to the LEFT. Watch the blue water molecules rush to the right side!
